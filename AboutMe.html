About Me

am a high achieving Physics Graduate with exceptional problem solving
skills and a competitive nature.I have a love for developing new
software and understanding complex theories in any field.

In my spare time I enjoy active sports such as beach volleyball and tennis.
I also have an appreciation for visiting new places and meeting new people.
My project at university was based on creating quantum well simulations. The
final simulation allowed the user to create shape quantum well, and find
the wave function for the created well.

Throughout my three years at university, I used a plethora of complex
apparatus to duplicate ground breaking experiments carried out by renowned
physicists from modern times. Understanding the procedure of these experiments
and the theory behind them has made me the confident problem solver I am today.
